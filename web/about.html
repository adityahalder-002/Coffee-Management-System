About us

Successfully Bringing smiles to faces and thoughts to minds...Since 2000

Back in 2000, in the Nasik city, when we were setting up this place, many people thought that it was a stupid idea. Afterall, setting up a coffee house at that time where people can only come and relax didn't sound quiet right. Nasik was extremely underdeveloped then.. So people thought that it was ridiculous to set up such a place. Now looking back towards it, I am extremely thankful to my parents that they didn't listen to 'those people' and continued to support me in their own unique ways. Slowly people were drawn to this place by its rich aroma and coziness. Today, along with my co-workers, there have also been many people who have been visiting this place very frequently. Some of the loyal customers have been with us for quiet some time now..Some since the start of this journey..They have grown and are living their fruitful lives in front of our very eyes. So, what makes our place so unique and exclusive?

Excellent quality at a competitive price: At Coffee Shop, we select our ingredients very carefully to ensure consistency and high-quality products at an affordable price to our consumers. Taste, hygiene and safety are our top priority. Innovative Products: We offer a wide variety of food and beverage products customized to suit our customers’ needs and preferences. Our product development team is continuously innovating and creating new tailored product lines. Excellent Service: Coffee Shop’s long history and experience in staff training along with serving different customer segments in a variety of sectors and formats, put us ahead of our competition in providing an outstanding and satisfying customer experience. Customer Focused: Our customer is our priority! Everything we do and stand for focuses on serving our customers and exceeding their expectations.
